The Problem

The coordination gap

Most organisations do not suffer from a lack of technology. They suffer from a lack of alignment.

A typical enterprise environment contains cloud platforms, private infrastructure, data platforms, security tooling, application portfolios, internal teams, external providers and emerging AI initiatives.

Each component may be effective individually. Few are designed to operate as a coordinated system.
