Purpose

Why this assessment exists

Modern technology estates evolve faster than governance, budgets and operating models.

Assessments create decision-ready clarity. They help leadership teams understand current maturity, identify gaps, prioritise action and move from opinion to evidence.

CoreCloud assessments are designed for executive engagement, not generic enquiry capture.

Evaluation

What we evaluate

Each assessment uses a structured framework aligned to CoreCloud methodology.

01

Economics

Consumption patterns, cost predictability, growth projections and long-term economics.

02

Performance

Latency, throughput, user experience and geographic considerations.

03

Sovereignty

Data residency, jurisdiction, regulatory obligations and governance requirements.

04

Security

Risk profile, access controls, operational security and compliance controls.

05

Operability

Skills availability, supportability, complexity and lifecycle management.

06

Innovation

AI readiness, data accessibility, platform flexibility and future scalability.
