The Executive Guide to Workload Venue Strategy.

How modern organisations determine the optimal venue for workloads, data, platforms and AI capabilities.

Executive Summary

Cloud is not the destination. Cloud is one venue. Modern organisations need a workload-first approach that balances economics, sovereignty, governance, performance, AI readiness and organisational capability.

CoreCloud Workload Venue Framework

  • Classify the workload.
  • Assess technology economics.
  • Assess sovereignty and control.
  • Assess operational requirements.
  • Select the optimal venue.

Venue Options

Public cloud, private cloud, sovereign cloud, colocation, edge and hybrid models all have valid roles. The correct venue depends on the workload, not the vendor narrative.

Executive Action Plan

Inventory critical workloads, classify by business criticality and data sensitivity, assess economics and sovereignty, then build a phased venue roadmap.
