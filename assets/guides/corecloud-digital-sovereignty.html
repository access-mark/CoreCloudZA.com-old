The Executive Guide to Digital Sovereignty.

How modern organisations maintain control in multi-cloud and AI-driven environments.

Executive Summary

Data residency answers where data is stored. Digital sovereignty answers who ultimately controls data, platforms, access, governance and recoverability.

CoreCloud Sovereignty Framework

  • Jurisdiction
  • Residency
  • Access
  • Control
  • Recoverability

Why It Matters

As organisations adopt cloud, SaaS and AI platforms, dependency increases. Sovereignty is the discipline of maintaining meaningful control as technology ecosystems become more distributed.

Executive Action Plan

Map critical data and workloads, assess jurisdictional exposure, review access pathways, evaluate recoverability and build a sovereignty roadmap.
